I am a researcher in the field of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), focusing on large language models (LLMs) and their applications in humanitarian negotiations, mental health support, and bias reduction.

My work centers on evaluating and improving how AI systems can be used responsibly and effectively in sensitive domains, with a particular emphasis on supporting underrepresented communities.

Prior to my current position, I completed my master's in Computational Science and Engineering from Harvard University, where I began exploring human-computer interaction for developers.
